I am a student of full stack web development at Austin Coding Academy. Before starting at Austin Coding Academy I taught bilingual pre-k and kindergarten for 6 years, and took on the role of Campus Innovation Coach at my school. This meant going out and researching various robotics and technology resources that our students could use in the classroom to learn or develop skills that they weren't getting from the curriculum. I discovered that I really enjoy robotics and coding, and also that there is a lack of apps available to bilingual students with special needs. I am passionate about finding ways to make technology more accessible for little kids, easier to implement for teachers, and ways to improve problem-solving and social emotional skills through collaboration.
